Living in the Pacific Northwest, we have a great opportunity to fish
for Salmon in our very own local rivers. Bellingham is home to one of
the better Salmon rivers in Washington, the Nooksack. Salmon probably
fight the hardest out of any fish that I've caught, and are some of the
bigger fish that I've caught.
